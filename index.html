GENERAL QUESTIONS

FAQ Glow Aesthetic

Find answers to frequently asked questions by our clients

The duration of treatment results varies depending on the type of treatment and the skin condition of each individual. For example:

  • Facial treatments usually last 2-4 weeks
  • Laser treatment can last 6-12 months with proper maintenance

Our doctor will provide specific information during consultation.

We use the latest technology and special techniques to minimize discomfort during treatment:

  • Most clients report feeling only a mild sensation
  • like being pricked by a very fine needle
  • For intensive treatment, we provide topical anesthesia

Recommended number of sessions depending on:

  • Kondisi kulit Anda saat ini
  • Treatment objectives to be achieved

We generally recommend a package of 3-6 sessions at specific intervals for lasting results. The doctor will make personalized recommendations after a thorough skin examination.

Preparation varies depending on the type of treatment. In general:

  • Avoid excessive sun exposure 1 week before treatment
  • Discontinue products containing retinoids/AHA/BHA 3 days in advance
  • Do not shave the treatment area 24 hours in advance

Our team will provide complete guidance after booking.

Possible side effects:

  • Redness or small swelling
  • Burning sensation (usually resolves within 24-48 hours)

We provide customized products and post-treatment care guidance . Our doctors are always on call for any concerns.
